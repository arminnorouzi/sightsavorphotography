Meet Our Team

Armin

Armin, with a Ph.D. in Engineering, is a Machine Learning Engineer whose passion lies at the intersection of advanced technology and photography. With a penchant for capturing timeless moments, he enjoys delving into the world of old-school photography, primarily using his trusty Canon camera. Beyond his professional pursuits, Armin finds solace and creativity through his lens, celebrating the beauty of life's fleeting instants. Connect with Armin on LinkedIn.

Sara

Sara, a software developer and ex-Googler, excels in her role with a Master's in Software Engineering. Her passion for nature photography shines through phone photography, capturing spontaneous beauty. Often complementing Armin's detailed shots, Sara's quick captures of nature's marvels showcase her unique perspective. Whether it's a missed moment by a fancy camera or a sudden burst of natural wonder, Sara is always ready to seize it. Connect with Sara LinkedIn.
